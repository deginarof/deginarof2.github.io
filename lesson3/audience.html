Audience

Who: Common people who are interested in daily forecast weather for work or leisure events. Age: 15 to 100 Technology: Ordinary people who owns a mobile or a computer. Income: Over $30,000 annually. Motivation: Apply the info from the page into decision making about short term project, weather personal or not.

Scenarios

Will it be okay to work outdoors?
Will the winds be too strong for a photo shooting?
Will the sun be enough to feed my solar energy system this week?
Will the weather be nice for a film session under the blankets with hot chocolate?
